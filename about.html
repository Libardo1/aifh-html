About the Series

Artificial Intelligence for Humans (AIFH) is a series of books that is currently under development. At this point I am writing Volume 1: Fundamental Algorithms. The plan is to release this volume sometime in December 2013. Volume 1 was launched through a successful Kickstarter campaign.

The book series teaches Artificial Intelligence concepts in a mathematically gentle manor. What exactly do I mean by mathematically gentle?

  • I assume the reader is proficient in at least one programming language.
  • I assume the reader has a basic understanding of College Algebra.
  • I do use topics and formulas from Calculus, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations and Statistics.
  • However, when explaining topics in bullet point 3, I always assume the reader is at the level of the first two bullets above.
  • I always follow concepts with real-world programming examples and pseudo code. Not just mathematical formulas.
  • The books stay at the pseudo code level.
  • Example packs are provided for: Java, C#, R, C/C++ and Python.
  • The community is encouraged to help port to other languages!
